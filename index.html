We are CZE Consulting - specialists in application development and systems integration with a focus on configuring Microsoft cloud technologies. Use our local knowledge and experience to get the most out of your technology.

How we work…

App Development

You and your clients need to be able to access your applications from anywhere and in every way. They need responsive, accessible designs that work on a variety of devices, from phones and tablets to full desktop experiences. We provide these experiences by using common modern web technologies including Javascript frameworks like Vue, React and Angular.

Systems Integration

It's not enough to have an application with a beautiful design that is a joy for humans to use, it also needs to work with other services. We have embraced the move away from monolithic systems and are specialists at API development and systems integration. We help your systems to work in concert with each other by building appropriate orchestration services and API endpoints.

Big Data

Just as you want to be able to use your applications everywhere, you want your data at your finger tips to help inform you and enable you to make the right decisions. Whether it's data about projects and their progress, financial and accounting data or some other analytics, we can help to capture, organise and present the data you need to see in the way you want to see it.

Cloud Solutions

We believe in the principle of cloud first. We configure solutions using SaaS products like Microsoft 365, SharePoint Online, Dynamics 365 and the Power Platform. We use Azure Cloud Computing Services due to it's flexibility and interoperability with both SaaS products and on premise Microsoft infrastructure like Active Directory, SQL Server and SharePoint.

Devops

Our cloud first principle means that we take an Infrastructure as Code approach when developing a solution. We believe in automating as much as possible including our CI/CD pipelines. We also use Azure DevOps to not only host these pipelines, but to provide end-to-end tracking of work items from inception right through the entire development lifecycle.

Methodology

We believe in the principles of the Manifesto for Agile Software Development, but above all we value adaptability. Our consultants are TOGAF certified and can operate as part of your Enterprise Architecture team, they can be deployed within your Scrum delivery teams or we can work with you directly. We adapt to your environment and the way you prefer to work.
